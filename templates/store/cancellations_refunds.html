Cancellations & Refunds

Last updated: March 2026

Order Cancellation

  • You can cancel your order before it is shipped. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.
  • To cancel, go to My Orders and use the Cancel Order option, or contact us at support@thezivo.com.
⚠️ Orders that have already been shipped cannot be cancelled. Please refuse delivery if you no longer want the order.

Refund Policy

We offer refunds in the following situations:

  • Order cancelled before shipment — full refund
  • Item received is damaged, defective, or significantly different from what was ordered
  • Item not delivered within the promised timeframe
We do not accept returns for reasons such as change of mind, wrong size ordered, or colour appearing different from the screen. Please check the size chart before ordering.

Return Process

To raise a return or refund request:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery at support@thezivo.com with your order ID and photos of the defective/incorrect item.
  2. Our team will review and approve the request within 2 business days.
  3. Once approved, ship the item back to our address. Return shipping is covered by us for defective items.
  4. Refund is processed after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Refund Timelines

  • Online payments (UPI / Card / Net Banking): Refund within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.
  • Cash on Delivery orders: Refund via bank transfer within 7–10 business days. We will request your bank details.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags/packaging
  • Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Sale or clearance items marked as final sale

Exchange Policy

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. Please place a new order for the correct size/item and raise a return request for the original order.

Contact Us

For any cancellation or refund queries, reach us at support@thezivo.com or visit our Contact Us page.
