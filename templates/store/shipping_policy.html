Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Delivery Charges

🎉 Free delivery on all orders above ₹799

A flat delivery charge of ₹59 applies to orders below ₹799. Delivery charges are non-refundable.

Delivery Timeframe

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of placement.
  • Estimated delivery: 5–7 business days across India.
  • Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata) may receive orders faster.
  • Remote or rural areas may take up to 10 business days.
Business days are Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays.

Order Processing

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM IST are processed the same day.
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM IST or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.
  • You will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking details once your order is dispatched.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you can track it by visiting My Orders. You will also receive a notification when your order is dispatched.

Delivery Coverage

We deliver across India. If delivery is unavailable to your pincode, you will be notified before your order is confirmed and a full refund will be issued.

Delivery Attempt & Failed Delivery

  • Our courier partner will make up to 3 delivery attempts.
  • After 3 failed attempts, the package will be returned to us.
  • For returned packages, a re-shipping fee may apply for re-dispatch.

Contact Us

For shipping-related queries, write to us at support@thezivo.com or visit our Contact Us page.
